Chicken Taco Recipe

Shredded chicken tacos

This recipe is the perfect, easy to make, delicious, at home DIY chicken taco recipe. With a nice toasted corn tortilla as the perfect, sturdy casing to hold all the ingredients, well-seasoned shredded chicken, and an acidic pico de gayo to cut through all the richness. I promise this recipe will not disappoint.

Ingredients

  • Skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoon taco seasoning mix
  • Corn tortilla
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Diced onions
  • Lime
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker
  3. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken
  4. Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours
  5. Shred chicken
  6. Heat corn tortilla on pan until crispy
  7. Dice onions and tomatoes
  8. Mix onions, tomatoes, and lime juice
  9. Add shredded chicken on tortilla
  10. Garnish with pico de gayo mix
  11. Add hot sauce if you like!
