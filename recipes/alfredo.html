Fettucine Alfredo Recipe

Creamy Fettucine Alfredo

A classic Italian staple, Fettucine Alfredo has consistently been one of the most popular dishes in the pasta world. With a rich, creamy sauce of heavy cream and cheese that coats the fettucine beautifully, this dish has been the staple of Italian restaurants around the world.

Ingredients

  • Skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoon taco seasoning mix
  • Corn tortilla
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Diced onions
  • Lime
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl
  2. Place chicken in a slow cooker
  3. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken
  4. Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours
  5. Shred chicken
  6. Heat corn tortilla on pan until crispy
  7. Dice onions and tomatoes
  8. Mix onions, tomatoes, and lime juice
  9. Add shredded chicken on tortilla
  10. Garnish with pico de gayo mix
  11. Add hot sauce if you like!
